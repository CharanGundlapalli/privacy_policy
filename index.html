Privacy Policy - Micro-Credential Aggregator

Last updated: March 2025

Welcome to Micro-Credential Aggregator, a platform designed to collect, manage, verify, and showcase digital micro-credentials and certificates issued by trusted organizations. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • User role (User / Issuer / Admin)
  • Profile details provided by you

1.2 Credential Information

  • Certificate title and type
  • Issuing organization
  • Issue and expiry dates
  • Credential ID
  • Uploaded certificate files (PDF)

1.3 Usage & Technical Data

  • IP address and device information
  • Login activity and timestamps
  • System logs for performance and security

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate and manage user accounts
  • Store and display verified credentials
  • Enable certificate verification
  • Manage issuer approvals and validations
  • Improve platform performance and security
  • Provide support and important notifications

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Secure server storage
  • Role-based access control
  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Regular monitoring and updates

4. Your Rights

  • View and update your profile information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Control visibility of shared credentials

5. Third-Party Services

  • Cloud storage services for files
  • Email services for notifications
  • Analytics tools to improve usability

All third-party services are required to follow strict data protection standards.

6. Children’s Privacy

Micro-Credential Aggregator is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the app and on our website.
